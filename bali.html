Exploring Bali

Bali is known for its beaches, temples, art, and peaceful atmosphere. It's a place I imagine visiting when I want a mix of relaxation, nature, and unique cultural experiences.

The island offers countless things to see—rice terraces, waterfalls, street food, local craft markets, and small villages full of traditional architecture. There's also a thriving yoga and meditation culture.

What I Want to Experience

  • Ubud’s rice terraces and art markets
  • The beaches of Uluwatu and Seminyak
  • Monkey Forest Sanctuary
  • Balinese temples like Tanah Lot and Ulun Danu

Travel Notes

Bali seems like the kind of place where each day can shift between calm and adventure. I'd want to spend the mornings exploring quiet temples, the afternoons by the ocean, and the evenings trying local food or watching traditional dance performances.

More planning will come later, but for now I’ve added enough content to fill the page while I keep learning about this beautiful island.
